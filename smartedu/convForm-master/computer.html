SCIENCE STREAM AFTER 10TH

Science Stream: A Brief

There are three branches of science: PCM, the non-medical branch, PCB, the medical branch, and PCMB. The primary difference between these streams is the third main subject, which is either Maths or Biology. PCMB offers both subjects. Alongside core subjects, candidates learn a first and second language. Let’s discuss each branch in detail:

PCM (Non-Medical)

If you aim to pursue engineering, PCM is for you. Physics, Chemistry, and Maths are the core subjects along with optional electives. Here's a brief outline:

Physics

In class 11, you’ll cover fundamental concepts and laws of physics. Major topics include units of measurement, motion, thermodynamics, gravitation, and waves. As you advance, the concepts get more engaging.

Chemistry

Chemistry introduces atoms, molecules, and chemical equations. Key chapters include equilibrium, thermodynamics, redox reactions, periodic properties, hydrocarbons, and organic chemistry.

Maths

Maths in class 11 may seem challenging initially, but with focus, you’ll master topics like trigonometry, quadratic equations, permutations, combinations, limits, and derivatives.

Optional Subjects

Besides core subjects, you can choose an optional elective. Popular choices include:

  • Economics
  • Computer Science
  • Home Science

Career Opportunities

After PCM, you can pursue:

  • Engineering
  • Architecture
  • Computer Applications
  • B.Sc in Physics/Chemistry/Maths

PCB (Medical)

If you aspire to be a doctor, PCB is the path. Besides Physics and Chemistry, Biology is included as the core subject. Key topics include cells, biomolecules, plant respiration, and human physiology.

Career Opportunities

Popular career options include:

  • Doctor (MBBS)
  • Pharmacist
  • Physiotherapist
  • Veterinarian

PCMB

PCMB offers both Maths and Biology, providing a versatile choice for students interested in both medical and non-medical fields.

Career Opportunities

This stream opens doors to:

  • Engineering
  • Medical Courses
  • Biotechnology
  • Alternative Medicine
